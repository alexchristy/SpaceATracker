Ice Comments

 

Welcome to the Scott Air Force Base Air Passenger Terminal

Scott AFB Passenger Terminal is only manned during Space-Required and Space-Available missions.

Due to local mission requirements, our terminal only opens the day of projected roll calls that are indicated on the 72-hour schedule located in the left-hand column (for Space-A) or pre-coordinated Space-R movements. Mission allowing, the 72-hour forecast will be updated every duty day (Mon-Fri) No Later Than (NLT) 1630L.

Space-Required Passengers

Space-Required Passengers (Duty Passengers) on travel orders are to check-in at the Passenger Service Counter. The counter opens for check-in 2 hrs prior to departure. Duty passengers must be in line NLT 1 hr 30 mins prior to scheduled departure. Those passengers who arrive after the NLT time will be re-directed to Customer Service for further re-booking information or as PCS orders instruct.

Space Available Passengers

At this time, Roll Calls are conducted at the Passenger Service Counter 2 hours and (30) mins prior to departure. It is highly encouraged that passengers arrive prior to roll call time to ensure all passengers are marked "Present" for their flight.

Space-Available Sign-Up is available in the following methods:

1. Using the automated Sign-Up available on the AMC’s Space Available Travel Page. Please follow the link provided in the "Quick Links" panel.

2. Scanning the QR code at the bottom of the "Contact Information" panel.

3. Email Passenger Sign-Up information to the organization box at scott.spacea@us.af.mil

The following information is required:

Email Address, Rank/Grade, Name, Branch of Service, Military Status, Name(s) of Dependent Travelers, Total Seat(s) Required, Travel Eligibility Status, Leave Dates, Overseas Travel-ready Status, Destination(s).

Space Available Reminders

Space Available seats are often limited, therefore, passenger planning and flexibility are key to Space Available travel. Please be advised that Active Duty members MUST be on leave status to sign up for Space-Available flights. 

There is no guarantee of transportation and reservations will not be accepted or made for any space-available traveler. The DoD is not obligated to continue an individual's travel or return the individual to the point of origin or any other point. Travelers should have sufficient personal funds to pay for commercial transportation, lodging, and other expenses if space-available transportation is not available. All associated expenses are the responsibility of the traveler. (Per DODI 4515.13, Section 4, Paragraph 4.1.a.)

For children under the age of ten years, without ID cards, passengers may use passports, or the child’s DoD ID number which can be retrieved from MilConnect website: https://milconnect-pki.dmdc.osd.mil/milconnect/. If the passenger ID card does not show a DoD ID number, the PSA must solicit it from the passenger. The DoD ID number may be retrieved from the MilConnect website. (Per AFI 24-605, Volume 2, Paragraph 2.25.3.)

Baggage Allowances

For most aircraft, passengers are authorized two (2) bags per person, (70) pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of (62) linear inches*. Additionally, passengers may have one (1) carry-on bag and one (1) personal item that MUST fit under any aircraft seat, not to exceed (45) linear inches. Please refer to the list below for specific aircraft limitations at Scott AFB.

  • C-21:  (1) CARRY-ON bag, not to exceed 30 lbs
  • C-40:  (2) CHECKED bags, not to exceed 50 lbs per bag
  • C-5, C-17, KC-135, and C-130:  (2) CHECKED bags, not to exceed 70 lbs per bag

For complete baggage requirements, please see an AMC passenger representative.

*Calculate linear inches by adding the bag length plus width plus height

Local Travel Information

The Scott AFB Air Transportation Function Terminal is located at 220 Heritage Drive, Bldg P8, Scott AFB, IL, 62225. We are located about 20 miles east of St. Louis, Missouri, near Belleville, Illinois. If needed, long-term parking is available for a period, not to exceed two weeks. 

 

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit.
