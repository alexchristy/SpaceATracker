Welcome to the Fairchild AFB Passenger Terminal

Duty Passengers (Space-Required Passengers)

Duty passengers must be in line NO LATER THAN (NLT) **2 hrs 20 mins** prior to scheduled departure. Those passengers who arrive after the NLT time will be re-directed to Customer Service for further re-booking information or as PCS/TDY orders instruct.

Duty passengers traveling on the Patriot Express(PE) flights are reminded that they can utilize the option to check-in no more than 24 hours prior to departure but limited to operating hours of the Passenger Terminal Early check in hours will be posted and advertised upon notice of departing PE flights.

Space Available Passengers

Roll calls are conducted at the AMC ticket counter 2 hours and 20 minutes prior to departure.

Space Available seats are often limited, therefor, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat.

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit.

Passengers who desire Space-A travel on flights with Roll Call times between 1500 to 0700, Monday through Friday, or any time on Weekends must let us know of their intent to travel no later than 1500 on the last duty day prior to the flight.

The Fairchild Passenger Terminal is only manned during Space-Required and Space-Available missions.

Local Travel Information

Flight Meals

Flight meals are available for purchase at the Warrior Dining Facility Flight Kitchen (100 West Bong St, Fairchild AFB, WA 99011). If a flight meal is desired, it is recommended to arrive to the Flight Kitchen 45 minutes prior to flight roll call. Cash and card are accepted.
