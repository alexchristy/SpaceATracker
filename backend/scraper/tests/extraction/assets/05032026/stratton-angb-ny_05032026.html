109th Space Available Program

Reminder To All Space-A Travelers: Per DODI 4515.13, Section 4, Paragraph 4.1.a, Reservations. There is no guarantee of transportation and reservations will not be accepted or made for any space-available traveler. The DOD is not obligated to continue an individual's travel or return the individual to the point of origin or any other point. Travelers should have sufficient personal funds to pay for commercial transportation, lodging and other expenses if space-available transportation is not available. All associated expenses are the responsibility of the traveler.

109th Logistics Readiness Squadron

"Implementing time-related positioning of resources for maximum effect and delivery."

Welcome to the 109th Airlift Wing's Space Available Travel webpage. The 109th Air Terminal Operations team continues to provide the best possible service and information to our travelers in order to make your travel preparations easier. Please take some time to explore the information provided on our site that will assist you in your planning. If for some reason you cannot find the information you need or have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact the Passenger Service section at the number provided, or you can click on the AMC logo for additional information.

Here at the 109th Logistics Readiness Squadron, it is our goal to assist you with your travel plans. Should you have the opportunity to pass through our terminal, I ask that you take some time to fill out a passenger customer service survey.

Enjoy your flight!
JAMES M. ROTH, Lt Col, NYANG
Commander, 109th Logistics Readiness Squadron
