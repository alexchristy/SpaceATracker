DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

***IMPORTANT AMC POLICY UPDATE***

EFFECTIVE AS OF 30 JUNE 2025

POLICY: Effective immediately all service members and dependents attempting to utilize DoD travel must adhere to the following requirements:

1. Dependents under the age of 10 must travel with the sponsor or eligible parent and possess a Federal-, State-, local-, or tribal government-issued identification IAW DoDI 4515.13. This does not include a local school ID unless it is issued by a government agency. A raised seal birth certificate is also valid from the city, county, state, etc. This does not include a hospital certificate of birth. A passport is also an example of identification if available.

2. U.S. USID sponsor will possess valid documentation showing proof of sponsorship of dependents for members under the age of 10 and traveling with the sponsor. Proof of sponsorship can be obtained through the installation’s Military Personnel Office or by accessing the following website: https://milconnect.dmdc.osd.mil/milconnect/. An example of sponsorship validation is the DD Form 1172-2.

a. The above proof of sponsorship is the only valid document accepted for travel. Other documentation, i.e., a birth certificate will not be accepted.

Welcome to the 732nd Air Mobility Squadron Passenger Terminal - home of the "Huskies". At Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson (JBER) Alaska, Huskies are committed to providing you the best possible customer service and our dedicated professionals take pride in what they do. Our primary destinations service Eielson (AK), Hickam (HI) & Travis (CA) Air Force Bases and Yokota (JP), Kadena (JP), Misawa (JP) & Osan (KR) Air Bases, with no regularly scheduled flights. For information regarding country clearances, please contact our customer service representatives. Members traveling on orders may contact 773rd Logistics Readiness Squadron Ground Transportation @ DSN 94-317-552-4475 or Commercial @ 907-552-4475 for transportation to/from billeting.  All others can contact a local taxi service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit to JBER. 

Duty Passengers

Duty passengers on Travel Orders must check in line NO LATER THAN (NLT) **3 hours ** prior to scheduled departure. Those passengers who arrive after the NLT time will be re-directed to Customer Service for further re-booking information.  

Duty passengers destined to Eareckson AS, AK (Shemya) are limited to two bags at 50 lbs each and no carry-on is allowed.

Duty Standby & Space Available Passengers

Roll calls are conducted at the AMC passenger service counter 3 hours prior to departure.  

Space Available seats are often limited, therefor, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat.

As a reminder, Federal Aviation Administration regulations prohibit passengers from drinking alcohol on board aircraft unless the air carrier serves it.

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit.
