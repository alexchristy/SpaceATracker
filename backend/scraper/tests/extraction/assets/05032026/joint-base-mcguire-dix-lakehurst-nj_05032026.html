Welcome to the Joint Base McGuire-Dix-Lakehurst Passenger Terminal

 

Duty (Space Required) Passengers

Passenger Service Counters are open for check-in up to 24 hrs prior to departure for Space-Required Duty Passengers. Duty passengers must be in line NO LATER THAN (NLT) roll call times listed on site. Those passengers who arrive after the NLT time will be re-directed to Customer Service for further re-booking information or as PCS orders instruct.

Duty Standby & Space Available Passengers

Space Available seats are often limited. Therefore, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat.

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit.

Local Travel Information

    • Proof of sponsorship can be obtained through the installation's Military Personnel Office. An example of sponsorship validation is the DD Form 1172-2.​
    • All dependents aged 14 and above must have an ID card IAW DoDI 4515.13. Dependents under 14 may qualify for air travel with alternative forms of identification if they don't have an issued ID card. ​
    • Identification must be issued in the Dependent's name from a Federal, State, Local, or Tribal government.​
    • A raised seal birth certificate from the city, county, state, etc is acceptable. This does not include a hospital certificate of birth. A passport is also an example of identification if available and only required when identified in the Foreign Clearance Guide.
