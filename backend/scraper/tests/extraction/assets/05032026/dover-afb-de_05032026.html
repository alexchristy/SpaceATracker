Welcome to the Dover Air Force Base Passenger Terminal

ATTENTION PASSENGERS:

For select flights, due to aircraft limitations, only 20 passengers will be authorized checked baggage. Passengers selected over 20 will be authorized carry-on baggage only. 

Mobile site is experiencing issues. Please try accessing schedule through a computer for complete information.

Our new hours will be 0800-2400, 7 days a week.

 

 

 

Duty Passengers

Duty passengers on Travel Orders are to check-in at the Passenger Service Counters located to your immediate right when you enter the terminal. Counters open for check-in 5 hrs prior to departure. Duty passengers must be in line NO LATER THAN (NLT) **3 hrs 20 mins** prior to scheduled departure. Those passengers who arrive after the NLT time will be re-directed to Customer Service for further re-booking information or as PCS orders instruct.

Duty Standby & Space Available Passengers

Roll calls are conducted at the AMC Passenger Services counter 3 hours prior to departure. It is recommended that passengers arrive approximately one or two hours prior to roll call time, in order to ensure time for processing.

Space Available seats are often limited, therefore, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat.

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit.

Remote Sign-Up


Sponsor's e-mail must include:

Name

Branch of Service

Rank

Duty Status (Active, Retired, Reserve, Guard, Dependent, etc.)

Leave Dates (if applicable)

and up to five countries that you would like to be signed up for. 

Please also include the information of any dependents that will be traveling with you. 

 

The following information is optional, but would save you time when you mark yourself present:

DoD ID Number

Date of Birth

Individual Body Weight

Cell Phone Number and Email Address (that you would like text notifications to go to as you travel)

Passport Information (if planning to travel overseas): Nationality, Passport Number, Country of Issue, Expiration Date.

Emergency Point of Contact Information (someone who is not traveling with you): Name and Phone Number
