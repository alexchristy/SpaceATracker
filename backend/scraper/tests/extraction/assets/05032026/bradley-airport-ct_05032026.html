Mission Statement

Deploy and employ air refueling, airlift, expeditionary aerospace and combat support forces around the world, while supporting National Security, Homeland Defense, and State of Maine objectives at home.

Vision Statement

To be a world-class organization, inspiring all to achieve their full potential, while building on the MAINEiac tradition of excellence, teamwork and commitment to the mission.

Commander's Priorities

- Reoptimize the Maine Air National Guard for maximum global impact
- Lead change with existing and emerging teammates
- Recruit and retain the nation's best
