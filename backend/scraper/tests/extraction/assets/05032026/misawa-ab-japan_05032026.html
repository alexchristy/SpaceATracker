WELCOME TO THE MISAWA PASSENGER SERVICE TERMINAL

Air Mobility Command, Misawa Air Terminal Ground Handling Services, and the 35th Fighter Wing welcome you to Japan. We hope the following information will assist you during your stay at Misawa Air Base. Please feel free to ask a Passenger Service Agent any questions you may have.

Space Available seats are often limited, therefore, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

As of  14 June 2024, the Misawa Passenger Terminal has officially gone cashless

FOR PASSENGERS TRAVELING ON ORDERS, TO CHECK-IN YOU MUST HAVE WITH YOU:

  • CARRY ON BAGS
  • CHECKED BAGS
  • PETS
  • PHYSICAL COPY OF ORDERS
  • MILITARY ID/PASSPORTS

CHECK-IN CANNOT HAPPEN WITHOUT ALL OF THESE ITEMS
