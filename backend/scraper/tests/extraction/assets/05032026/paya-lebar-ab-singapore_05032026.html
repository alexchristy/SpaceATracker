Welcome

Welcome to the Paya Lebar Passenger Terminal, the “Pride of the Pacific”. We pride ourselves in providing superior customer service to all our patrons and visitors.

We are located on Paya Lebar Air Base, in Singapore.  (Republic of Singapore Air Force Installation)

Space Available seats are often limited, therefore, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

On behalf of Detachment 2, 730th Air Mobility Squadron, and Air Mobility Command (AMC), we wish you an enjoyable travel experience and look forward to assisting you in future travels. Please let us know if you've any questions or concerns during your visit to the Paya Lebar Passenger Terminal.


Directions

Turn right off Airport Road (Eastern Link) at Paya Lebar Air Base entrance and follow signs for “Visitors and Drop off Point”/ Straight ahead to the Pass Office / Proceed to follow foot traffic through initial base security checkpoint / Follow sidewalk after security until the street crossing and AMC Terminal is in the “Air Movements Center” building across the street.

Duty Standby & Space Available Passengers

Duty Passengers are passengers that are booked through a government travel office such as TMO/SATO typically for PCS, TDY, and Deployment purposes.

Roll calls are conducted 3 hours prior to scheduled departure. 

Space Available seats are often limited, therefore, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.  

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item that must fit under any aircraft seat.

***We are going cashless from 1 May 2024.We will only be accepting card payment for all the transactions to include excess baggage.***

For any further questions about the AMC travel guidance, please refer to the quick links listed on this page.

Local Travel Information

Singapore Entrance Requirements

Passports must have minimum 6-month validity.

All travelers must fill out the online SG arrival card within 3 days of arrival.  Completion of arrival card is tied to your entry documentation.  Immigration is not possible without completing arrival card.


For Country Entry Requirements

Please reference the Foreign Clearance Guide for country entry requirements or contact your local passenger terminal.


Base and Terminal Amenities

Our Passenger Terminal offers free Wi-Fi, Distinguished Visitors Lounge, and beverage vending machine (accepts SGD).  Food options are not available to passengers once on Paya Lebar Air Base.  No ATMs are located on Paya Lebar Air Base.
