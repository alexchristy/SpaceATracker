Our normal scheduled flights serve; Mildenhall, UK, MacDill AFB FL, Hickam AFB HI, Travis AFB CA, Fairchild AFB WA, and Elmendorf AFB AK.

Space-Required Passengers

Duty passengers on Travel orders are to check-in at any available counter. Counters open for check-in 6 hrs prior to departure. Duty passengers must be in line NO LATER THAN (NLT) ** 3hrs 20 min prior to scheduled departure. Passengers who arrive after the NLT time will be re-directed to Customer Service for further re-booking information.

 Space-Available Passengers

The counter will open 3 hours prior to departure. Space-A seats are often limited therefore passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space-A travel.

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat. 

Passengers are required to bring own food or coordinate meals with Flight Kitchen at least 4 hours prior to take off. Passenger Service Agents are not responsible for flight meals. 

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have questions or concerns during your visit.

Short/long-term parking.

Short/long term parking available outside of building 1220.

After Hours Roll Call Policy

The following procedures only apply to Roll Call times outside our normal duty hours. Passengers are not required to call if the Roll Call time is within our normal duty hours, Mon-Fri 0730-1600.

Passengers who desire Space-A travel on flights with Roll Call times after duty hours, or anytime on weekends and Holidays must let us know of their intent to travel no later than 1500 the duty day prior to the flight.

To inform us of your intent to travel, please call commercial (316) 759-4810 or DSN 759-4810. When you do call, please indicate the destination and date you wish to travel and a passenger service agent will be scheduled to process that flight. For those flights with no interested passengers, the flight will be removed from advertisement by 1500 the duty day prior to the flight, and a Roll Call will not be held.
