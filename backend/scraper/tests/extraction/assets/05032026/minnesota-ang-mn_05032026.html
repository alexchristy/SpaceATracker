SIGN-UP INSTRUCTIONS/GUIDE

Note: If you are considering traveling using Space-A, remember there is no guarantee you will be selected for a seat. Space-A travelers must be prepared to cover commercial travel expenses if flight schedules are changed or seats become unavailable (Per DODI 4515.13, Section 4, Paragraph 4.1.a)

 

  • Can I travel Space-A?: Utilize the Space-A Travel Eligibility Menu at the bottom of this page to ensure you fall in one of the 6 (VI) categories that are eligible to travel Space-A.
  • How to Sign-Up?: The easiest way to get your foot in the door, is to email us at 133.aw.space.a.org@us.af.mil with your sign-up info as listed below directly or by using the Sign Up Form below. This will put a timestamp on your sign-up right away.
    • Note: Your sign-up will be good from the start and end of leave/eligibility time frame or 90 days from signup for retirees.
    • PRO TIP!- For each stop and arriving destination, also send a sign-up to their terminals to ensure you have the earliest sign up for your return. Utilize the AMC Space A page for contact info of other stations.

    • Reminder: At no time is a Space-A seat guaranteed and all flights are subject to change without prior notice.

Long Term Parking

All Space-A passengers leaving a vehicle behind are required to park in our long term parking area. Please ensure to receive a parking permit at any time of day from the front gate at no charge prior to the start of Roll Call.

Meals

Meals will not be offered or provided on any flight. Water will be provided on flight. Passengers are encouraged to bring their own food and water bottles which can be filled up after security.

Space-A Travel Eligibility (from DODI 4515.13)

  • Category I - Emergency Leave Unfunded Travel
  • Category II - Accompanied EML
  • Category III - Ordinary Leave, Relatives, House Hunting Permissive TDY, MoH Holders & Foreign Military
  • Category IV - Unaccompanied EML
  • Category V - Permissive TDY (Non-House Hunting), Students, Dependents, Post Deployment/Mobilization Respite Absence & Others
  • Category VI - Retired, Dependents, Reserve, ROTC, NUPOC, CEC, Veterans w/ a Permanent Service-connected Disability Rated as Total, & Surviving Spouses of Service Members who Died in Active Duty, Inactive Duty Training, or Annual Training Status

Glossary

Marked Present: Passengers must be marked present prior to the start of Roll Call. Anyone marked present after the start of roll call will be placed at the bottom of the Roll Call list regardless of date/time of sign-up and category.

Roll Call Selection: Roll Call is the process of selecting passengers for a flight in the order of Category then Date/Time of sign-up.
