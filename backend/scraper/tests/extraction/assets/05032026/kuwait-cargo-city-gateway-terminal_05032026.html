Welcome to the AMC Kuwait - Cargo City Gateway

Outbound Duty Passengers

Outbound (re-deploying) duty passengers on Travel Orders are to check with their respective gateways for check-in times and processing. In most cases Air Force will be processed at Ali Al Salem AB and all other services will be processed at Camp Arifjan. 

Patriot Express passengers that are being processed at Camp Arifjan will go to the Passenger Travel office for booking/reservation, check-in times and processing procedures.

Camp Arifjan Outbound Terminal DSN - 318-480-2196 

Camp Arifjan Passenger Travel Office - 318-480-7515

Duty Standby & Space Available Passengers

*Note* KWI can only accept Active Duty and authorized DOD Civilians on orders for Space A travel. There is no Kuwaiti Immigration at KWI/OKKK (Cargo City). All contractor and civilian (dependent/retiree) travel must be conducted through Kuwait City International Airport (KCIA). For any other travel concerns please go to  The Electronic Foreign Clearance Guide (FCG) (pentagon.mil) for updated travel guidance.

Passengers will need to check with your respective gateways for availability, check-in times and processing.

Space Available seats are often limited, therefor, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel. 

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat.

As of current the Head tax and Federal Inspection Fee to enter the United States is $41.63 and can be paid with debit card or cash. We do not accept credit card or Eagle Cash card.

Inbound Passengers

In most cases travel from Cargo City is pre-coordinated prior to flight arrival.

For Patriot Express Passengers Air Force passengers will travel to Ali Al Salem AB for in country processing and all other services will travel to Camp Arifjan for in country processing unless prior approval is given. For further questions contact the Passenger terminal.
