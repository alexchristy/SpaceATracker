  Our mission: Provide mission-ready C-17 airlift, aeromedical evacuation and agile combat support.

Established: 15 July 1943
Total Force Strength: Approx. 1,500
Economic Impact (FY24): Approx. $2.62 Million
Aircraft in Inventory: Eight C-17 aircraft are currently assigned.
Distinctive Capabilities: Aeromedical Evacuation; Strategic Airlift; Agile Combat Support
Awards: 2015 AF Reserve Installation Excellence, 2024 Rain Cross Award Winners

 

ISO Dock: Isochronal Inspection process takes C-17 into in-depth maintenance from nose to tail. Maintenance revamped process from 60 day to 17 day turn-around – benchmarked across the Air Force.

JA/ATT:  Joint Airborne/Air Transportability Training missions flown year-round providing real war-time readiness for all military branches

Tenant Units: Navy Operational Support Center

Strategic Location: National Disaster Medical System hub for transporting patients during a disaster
