Offutt AFB Air Passenger Terminal

 

 

Welcome to the 55th Logistics Readiness Squadron Small Air Terminal at Offutt AFB, Nebraska.

The Offutt AFB Small Air Terminal is committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit with us.

Space Available flights are limited, therefore passenger planning and flexibility is key for Space Available travel out of Offutt AFB

Terminal Information

55th LRS Passenger Terminal
602 Looking Glass Blvd.
Offutt AFB, NE 68123

24-Hour Flight Recording:
DSN 271-7111
Commercial 402-294-7111

Customer Service:
DSN 271-2228
Commercial 402-294-2228

E-mail: 55lrs.Offutt.SpaceA@us.af.mil

Hours of Operation: Monday-Friday, 7:30 a.m. - 4:30 p.m.

For Space-A information visit: https://www.amc.af.mil/Home/AMC-Travel-Site/

Please send the following required information to the above email:

- Email address
- Rank/Grade
- First Middle Last Name
- Service branch
- Military status
- Name(s) of dependent travelers
- Total seats required
- Travel eligibility status (Category type)
- Leave dates
- Overseas travel-ready status (Required documents in-hand?)
- Destination(s) (Sign up for up to five destinations)
 

Please Do Not Provide Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as Social Security numbers.

 

Did You Know:

Portable devices such as a Hoverboard with Lithium Batteries can cause a fire?

Portable Lithium Battery Self-Powered Vehicles, also known as Hoverboards, Balance Wheels, etc., are NOT authorized on any DOD owned or controlled aircraft including Commercial Patriot Express and Military aircraft.  Safety concerns and possible overheating of these devices necessitate the restriction of travelling with these items by air.  Devices are not authorized in checked or carry-on baggage.

FYI: Electronic-Cigarettes must be in carry-on luggage and not in checked baggage due to the batteries used to operate them.
