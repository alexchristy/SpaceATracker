AIR FORCE TOP STORIES

185th Wing Commander

.
Welcome to the 185th Air Refueling Wing. As a KC-135 tanker unit the 185th ARW is involved missions on behalf of U.S. combatant commands including U.S. Strategic Command supporting Nuclear Deterrence, Global Reach mobility and Global Power missions around the world.
As part of the National Guard the 185th also stands ready to respond to state emergencies. The professionalism of our Airmen is a direct reflection of the people of the Siouxland community.
Since our beginnings, the unit has enjoyed unprecedented community support. The support of our communities enables our members to stand ready when needed. Please use this website to learn more about the 185th ARW mission and our people.
