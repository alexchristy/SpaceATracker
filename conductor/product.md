# SpaceATracker: Military Flight Discovery & Extraction

## Initial Concept
SpaceATracker is a modern system designed to discover military passenger terminals and extract their associated documents (schedules, reports) using a robust background scraper. It aims to bridge the gap between military flight data and the travelers who need it.

## Product Vision
To become the definitive, real-time resource for Space-A travelers and retirees, providing global coverage and deep historical insights into military flight availability. SpaceATracker will transform raw terminal data into an accessible, searchable, and actionable platform.

## Target Audience
- **Space-A Travelers:** Active duty personnel, dependents, and families looking for efficient travel options.
- **Retirees:** Veterans and their families who utilize Space-A travel for global transportation.

## Core Features
- **Flight-First Search UI:** A responsive dashboard where users interact directly with **flight listings** rather than just static documents.
- **Flight Data Extraction:** Intelligent extraction of individual flights from schedules to provide structured, searchable data.
- **Notification System:** Real-time alerts for specific flight routes, destinations, or new terminal schedules.
- **Source Backlinking:** Every flight listing is linked to its original source document (PDF, image) for easy user verification.
- **Transparency Dashboard:** Clear visibility into scraper status, including when each terminal's schedule was last checked.

## User Interaction & Data Access
- **Direct Flight Interaction:** Users search for and interact with specific flights based on destination, terminal, and date.
- **Verification Traceability:** High transparency is maintained by linking every extracted flight back to its original source schedule.
- **Scraper Visibility:** Real-time information on data freshness, allowing users to trust the platform's reliability.

## Long-Term Goals
- **Global Coverage:** Expanding the scraper to support all major military passenger terminals worldwide.
- **Historical Insights:** Leveraging extracted data to analyze trends in flight availability and seat types over time, helping travelers plan better.
