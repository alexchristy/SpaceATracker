# SpaceATracker: Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Concise & Informative:** Content should be clear and direct, providing high-signal information without unnecessary fluff.
- **Professional & Approachable:** Maintain a professional tone that is easy to understand, avoiding overly complex jargon where possible.

## UX Principles
- **High Clarity:** Every action and piece of information should be unmistakable, providing immediate understanding and feedback to the user.
- **Consistent Interactions:** Ensure a uniform design and behavior across the entire platform to build user familiarity and trust.
- **Mobile Accessibility:** The platform must be fully functional and easy to navigate on mobile devices, ensuring travelers can access data on the go.

## UI Aesthetics & Visual Style
- **Readability-Focused:** Prioritize high-contrast elements, clear typography, and a strong visual hierarchy to ensure data is easy to parse.
- **Mobile-First Design:** Focus on responsive layouts that adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes, with a primary emphasis on mobile usability.

## User Feedback & Transparency
- **Operational Transparency:** Be extremely clear when issues occur that affect data freshness. Users should be informed of scraper status and any potential delays in real-time updates.
- **Actionable Error Messages:** When errors occur, provide clear explanations and next steps to support user confidence and platform reliability.
